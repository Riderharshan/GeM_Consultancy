# 🚀 GeM Consultancy Website

A professional web platform designed to provide **end-to-end consultancy services** for buyers and sellers on the Government e-Marketplace (GeM).  
It helps businesses, startups, institutions, and government entities simplify procurement processes with ease.

---

## 🌐 Overview

The GeM Consultancy Website bridges the gap between users and complex government procurement systems.  
It provides structured guidance and expert support — from registration to successful order execution.

The platform ensures a **smooth, transparent, and efficient experience** for all users.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🛒 Seller Services
- GeM Seller Registration  
- Brand Approval & OEM Registration  
- Product Listing & Catalog Management  
- Bid Participation & Tender Filing  
- Order Processing & Compliance Support  

### 🏢 Buyer Services
- Buyer Registration Support  
- Tender Creation Assistance  
- BOQ Preparation  
- Vendor Evaluation  
- Contract & Order Management  

### 🎓 Educational Institutions
- GeM Procurement Support  
- Infrastructure & Equipment Procurement  
- Vendor Selection Guidance  
- Documentation Assistance  

### 🧠 Additional Services
- PFMS Support  
- ATL Lab Setup Guidance  
- Documentation & Compliance  
- Patent, Copyright & Design Registration  
- Tender Alerts & Strategy Guidance
